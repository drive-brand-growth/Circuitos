#### **JUDGMENT #1: LIQUID CASH REALITY**
**Universal Question:** Do they have liquid cash available NOW for this investment?

**Vertical-Specific Thresholds:**

| Vertical | Platinum (10pts) | Gold (7pts) | Silver (4pts) | Bronze (2pts) |
|----------|------------------|-------------|---------------|---------------|

| **Enterprise SaaS** | $250K+ annual contract | $100K-$250K | $50K-$100K | <$50K (SMB) |

**Why It's Universal:**
- ML data shows liquid cash is **ALWAYS** the #1 predictor (0.35-0.50 feature importance across ALL verticals)
