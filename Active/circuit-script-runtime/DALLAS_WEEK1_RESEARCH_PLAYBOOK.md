## üîç Research Tools & Resources

### **Owner Name Research:**
1. **Texas Secretary of State Business Search:**
   - URL: https://mycpa.cpa.state.tx.us/coa/
   - Search gym name
   - Look for LLC/Corporation filing
   - Note: Registered agent listed (often the owner)

2. **LinkedIn:**
   - Search: "[GYM NAME] owner Dallas"
   - Search: "[GYM NAME] founder"
   - Filter by location: Dallas, TX
   - Check company pages (employees listed)

3. **Gym Website:**
   - Check "About" or "Team" pages
   - Look for owner/founder bio
   - Check blog posts (often signed by owner)

### **Contact Info Research:**
1. **Email (Priority Order):**
   - Check website footer (info@, contact@, owner name@)
   - LinkedIn profile (sometimes listed)
   - Use Hunter.io (free 25 searches/month): https://hunter.io/
   - Use RocketReach (free trial): https://rocketreach.co/

2. **Phone:**
   - Google Business Profile
   - Website contact page
   - Facebook page "About" section
   - Yelp listing

### **Social Media Research:**
1. **Instagram:**
   - Search gym name
   - Search owner name (personal account may have more followers)
   - Check bio for other accounts (owner's personal)
   - Note follower count, post frequency, engagement

2. **TikTok:**
   - Search gym name
   - Check video views (indicator of reach)
   - Note if owner is featured in content

3. **Facebook:**
   - Search gym name
   - Check page likes
   - Look for owner personal profile link

### **Facility Size Estimation:**
1. **From photos:**
   - Count squat racks (1 rack = ~100-150 sq ft including walkways)
   - 10 racks = ~1,500 sq ft minimum
   - Add space for warmup, bathrooms, office = 1.5-2x rack space
   - Example: 10 racks = 3,000-4,000 sq ft total

2. **From lease info:**
   - Sometimes listed on commercial real estate sites
   - Check LoopNet history (if gym moved to this space)
