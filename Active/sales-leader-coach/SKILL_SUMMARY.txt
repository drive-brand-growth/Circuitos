WHAT IT DOES
================================================================================

Transforms lost sales conversations into winning strategies by:

1. ANALYZING CONVERSATIONS (Single or Batch up to 50)
   - Extracts pain points, buying signals, objections, gaps
   - Classifies conversation stage (Discovery → Closing)
   - Detects Eugene Schwartz awareness level (Unaware → Most Aware)
   - Assesses BANT/MEDDIC qualification status

2. APPLYING ELITE SALES METHODOLOGIES
   - MEDDIC/MEDDPICC (Enterprise qualification)
   - SPIN Selling (Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-Payoff)
   - Challenger Sale (Teach, Tailor, Take Control)
   - Gap Selling (Quantify current vs. future state)
   - + 8 more frameworks (Sandler, Value Selling, etc.)

3. MAPPING TO DRNSIGHTS PRODUCT KNOWLEDGE
   - Vehicle Search (Real-time LPR for repossession)
   - Skip Tracing ("Realternative data" for debt collection)
   - Risk Scoring (Alternative data for credit risk models)
   - Loss Alerts (Theft, impound, salvage monitoring)

4. GENERATING HUMANIZED RESPONSES
   - World-class responses that sound like a senior sales executive
   - Uses contractions, empathy, storytelling, data-driven insights
   - Applies Eugene Schwartz awareness-based messaging
   - Creates urgency without pressure

5. PROVIDING TACTICAL COACHING
   - What rep did well vs. what was missed
   - Specific actions to take next time
   - Which sales methodology to use
   - How to handle objections better

6. OUTPUTTING SALESFORCE AGENTFORCE-READY DATA
   - Structured JSON for Data Cloud enrichment
   - Auto-generated Knowledge Base articles
   - Playbook updates (trigger → action patterns)
   - Performance metrics and coaching priorities

   Date: YYYY-MM-DD
   Rep: [Name]
   Prospect: [Name], [Title], [Company]
   Stage: [Discovery | Demo | Proposal | etc.]
   Transcript: [Full conversation]
   Outcome: [Result]

2. BATCH CSV (Up to 50 conversations, 25 MB max)
   conversation_id, date, rep_name, prospect_name, prospect_title,
   prospect_company, conversation_stage, transcript, outcome

3. COPY-PASTE MULTIPLE (Delimited by "--- CONVERSATION X ---")

   [Metadata + Transcript]

   [Metadata + Transcript]
   --- END ---
