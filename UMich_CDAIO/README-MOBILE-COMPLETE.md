# Complete Executive AI Leadership Mobile Platform

## Overview

This mobile platform combines the **Michigan CDAIO Technical Training** (58 hours, 12 sessions) with the **MBA Education for AI Leaders** (44 hours, 16 modules) into a single, unified mobile learning experience.

**Total Content**: 102 hours across 28 modules

## Usage Instructions

### For Students

**Step 1**: Open `index-complete.html` on your mobile device
- Works on iPhone, iPad, Android phones and tablets
- Add to home screen for app-like experience

**Step 2**: Choose your learning path
- **CDAIO First** (Recommended): Complete 12 CDAIO sessions, then explore MBA modules
- **Parallel**: Alternate between CDAIO and MBA for integrated learning
- **MBA On-Demand**: Complete CDAIO, use MBA modules as reference

**Step 3**: Track your progress
- Each track has separate progress tracking
- Complete sessions to unlock next modules
- Progress saved in browser localStorage

### For Instructors/Administrators

**Deployment Options**:
1. **GitHub Pages**: Push to gh-pages branch
2. **Static Hosting**: Deploy to Netlify, Vercel, or AWS S3
3. **LMS Integration**: Embed in Canvas, Blackboard, or Moodle
4. **Mobile App**: Wrap in React Native or Capacitor

**Customization**:
- Edit HTML for styling and layout
- Modify `sessionScripts` object to add/update content
- Adjust progress tracking logic
- Add assessment quizzes

## Learning Outcomes

### CDAIO Track Outcomes
After completing CDAIO training, students will be able to:
- Understand AI/ML fundamentals and modern architectures
- Develop enterprise data strategies
- Design AI governance frameworks
- Lead AI/data organizations
- Communicate technical concepts to executives
- Navigate AI ethics and compliance

### MBA Track Outcomes
After completing MBA modules, students will be able to:
- Apply strategic frameworks to AI business problems
- Build financial models for AI investments
- Evaluate technology investment decisions
- Lead digital transformation initiatives
- Communicate AI value to boards and C-suite
- Design AI governance and risk management frameworks

- **T-shaped leaders**: Deep technical expertise + broad business acumen

## Certification Structure

### Option 1: CDAIO Certificate (58 hours)
- Complete all 12 sessions
- Pass session quizzes (80%+)
- Complete capstone project
- **Credential**: Certified Chief Data & AI Officer (C-CDAIO)

### Option 2: MBA Module Certificates (11-12 hours each)
- Complete individual MBA pillars
- Pass module assessments (70%+)
- **Credentials**: Certificate in AI Business Strategy, Data-Driven Decision Making, Technology Investment, or Digital Transformation

### Option 3: Complete EMBA-CDAIO (102 hours)
- Complete CDAIO Certificate
- Complete all 4 MBA pillars
- Pass integrated capstone project
- **Credential**: Executive MBA for Chief Data & AI Officers (EMBA-CDAIO)
