AI Past, Present and Future
Duration: 30 minutes
Explore the evolution of artificial intelligence from symbolic systems to modern foundation models. Understand machine learning fundamentals, current AI capabilities and limitations, and the transformative impact of large language models and generative AI.

Learning Objectives

  • Trace the evolution of AI from symbolic systems to foundation models
  • Understand machine learning fundamentals (supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement learning)
  • Assess current AI capabilities and limitations
  • Explore foundation models like GPT, BERT, and Stable Diffusion
  • Identify AI applications transforming industries

Topics Covered

  • History of AI: Symbolic AI to neural networks
  • Machine learning paradigms and algorithms
  • Deep learning and neural network architectures
  • Foundation models and transformer architecture
  • Generative AI (GPT, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion)
  • Current AI capabilities and real-world applications
  • AI limitations and challenges
  • Future trends in AI development

Transfer Learning & Foundation Models
Duration: 30 minutes
Understand how foundation models revolutionize AI by enabling transfer learning across tasks. Learn about pre-training and fine-tuning strategies, prompt engineering, and how to leverage models like GPT, BERT, and CLIP for your organization.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand transfer learning and its business value
  • Learn pre-training and fine-tuning strategies
  • Master prompt engineering techniques
  • Explore foundation model architectures (GPT, BERT, CLIP)
  • Apply foundation models to business problems

Topics Covered

  • Transfer learning fundamentals
  • Pre-training on large-scale data
  • Fine-tuning for specific tasks
  • Prompt engineering and in-context learning
  • GPT family (GPT-3, GPT-4, ChatGPT)
  • BERT and language understanding
  • Multimodal models (CLIP, DALL-E)
  • Foundation model business applications
